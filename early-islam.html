Early Islam in America

The story of Islam in America did not begin in the twentieth century. Muslims were present much earlier, including among enslaved Africans brought to America. Though many were stripped of language, family, and religious practice, their presence remains an important part of the American story.

This section introduces visitors to the deep historical roots of Islam in America and challenges the idea that Islam is only a recent arrival in the United States.

Exhibit Room 3

Malcolm X and Transformation

Malcolm X stands as one of the most influential Muslim figures in American history. His life reflected the struggle for justice, dignity, and truth. His pilgrimage to Mecca marked a major transformation in his religious understanding and public message.

This exhibit presents Malcolm X not only as a political figure, but as a Muslim whose journey reshaped conversations about race, brotherhood, and Islam in America.

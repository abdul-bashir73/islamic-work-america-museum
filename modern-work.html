Exhibit Room 4

Modern Islamic Work in America

Islamic work in America today includes masjid development, youth work, education, dawah, social services, community leadership, and public engagement. Muslim communities across the country continue to build institutions that serve both their own communities and the wider society.

This final exhibit highlights continuity: the work of preserving faith, building communities, and serving others remains central to Islamic life in America.

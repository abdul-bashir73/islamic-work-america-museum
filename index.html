Welcome to the Exhibit

Malcolm X and the transformation of Islamic identity in America

The Kaaba represents unity, direction, and purpose in Islam

This digital museum explores the history of Islamic work in America through struggle, transformation, and service. From early foundations to modern movements, each section reveals how faith shaped identity, resistance, and community building.

Move through the exhibit to understand how individuals and movements carried Islam forward in different eras, and how that legacy continues today.

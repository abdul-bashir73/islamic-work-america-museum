Welcome to the Exhibit

This digital museum explores the history of Islamic work in America, from its earliest roots to its development through struggle, reform, community building, and public service.

Visitors move through the exhibit as a historical journey: from early Muslim presence in America, to Black Muslim identity movements, to Malcolm X’s transformation, and finally to modern Islamic work through education, community leadership, dawah, and service.

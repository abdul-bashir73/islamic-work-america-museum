Welcome to the Exhibit

Faith • Identity • Struggle • Service

This museum site tells the story of Islamic work in America as a journey of faith, identity, struggle, and service. It is not just a list of facts. It is designed as a guided experience that helps visitors understand how Islam developed, transformed, and inspired communities in America.

Each exhibit focuses on a different stage: early Muslim presence, the Nation of Islam, Malcolm X’s transformation, and modern Islamic work through education, dawah, leadership, and service.

Design Direction

The visual style is inspired by editorial modernism: clean structure, museum-like spacing, strong typography, and a calm color palette. The brand archetype is the Sage, because the site is meant to teach, guide, and help visitors reflect. The persuasion principle is consistency, because each page encourages the visitor to continue through the story step by step.
